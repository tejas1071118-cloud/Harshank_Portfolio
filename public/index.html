ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Harshank Lokhande — a freelancer and a student at Viva Institute of Applied Art.

Art has been a part of my life since childhood, which is why I'm pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Fine Arts. Alongside my studies, I work as a freelancer specializing in Photography, Videography, and Video Editing.

This website showcases my video editing work — the space where my creative energy finds its truest expression.

I like to think of myself as a born artist — not someone naturally gifted, but someone who's always exploring, making mistakes, learning, and growing. I don't shy away from failure. I embrace it, learn from it, and move forward with more clarity and determination.

While I may not have years of experience just yet, I bring with me enthusiasm, curiosity, and a deep commitment to producing impactful, original content. When you work with me, you're not just hiring a freelancer — you're collaborating with someone who's genuinely passionate about solving creative problems and bringing ideas to life.
